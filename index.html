ABOUT

I am a media artist driven by the power of visuals to tell meaningful stories. My creative world blends animation, motion graphics, photography, video and graphic design, where every frame and composition becomes a language of its own. I explore how art and technology intersect to shape experiences that educate, inspire, and preserve culture. From crafting 2D and 3D animations to exploring Virtual and Augmented Reality (VR/AR), my goal is to create work that connects people, amplifies ideas, and reimagines how stories from Ghana and beyond are seen and felt.
